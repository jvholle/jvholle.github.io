Manage ArcGIS Online
user content

Temporary accounts in ArcGIS Online accumulate orphaned maps, layers, and other content. Script iterates through a list of users and their content, and attempts to remove relationships between groups, layers, and apps in order to delete items. If relationships are not removed and items cannot be directly deleted, the content is reassigned to an admin and deleted.
